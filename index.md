I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Research School of Astronomy & Astrophysics (RSAA) of the Australian National University. My primary research interest is the magnetised interstellar medium in the Milky Way and nearby galaxies.

My work often involves using world-class radio telescopes such as the Australian Square Kilometre Array Pathfinder (ASKAP), the Australia Telescope Compact Array (ATCA), and the Very Large Array (VLA).

This website is currently a work in progress.
